
The Winning Formula – Product Requirements Document (PRD)Owner: OriannePhase: Phase 1 – Beehiiv-FirstPrimary Platform: BeehiivTime Horizon: First 90 Days1. ObjectiveBuild a data-driven newsletter that demonstrates how data informs decision-making across property, F1, skincare, and technology. The goal is to build an engaged audience and validate demand before building full tools and a website.2. Core Pillars- Property & Assets- F1 & Performance- Skincare & Consumer Data- Tech, Data & Modeling3. Property & AssetsGoal:Teach readers how to think about property investment decisions using data.Talking Points:- Evaluating a suburb using data- Why averages are misleading- Supply vs demand indicators- ROI assumptions- Renovation trade-offsExamples:- Why great rental yields fail under comparison- How I evaluate a suburb before viewing listingsPhase 1 Output:- 2–3 newsletter issues- Simple ROI models (Excel/Sheets screenshots)4. F1 & PerformanceGoal:Use F1 as an engaging way to explain performance analysis through data.Talking Points:- Car evolution- Strategy vs results- Driver performance trends- Regulation impactsExamples:- Why this upgrade mattered more than pole position- What lap-time data reveals beyond race standingsPhase 1 Output:- 1–2 issues- Static charts and visuals5. Skincare & Consumer DataGoal:Show how data reshapes skincare trends and product understanding.Talking Points:- Ingredient trends- Product popularity cycles- Consumer sentiment vs hypeExamples:- Why this ingredient is trending- What product data says vs influencer claimsPhase 1 Output:- 1 issue- Tie-in to skincare app insights6. Tech, Data & ModelingGoal:Explain how data models are built and evaluated without intimidation.Talking Points:- When simple models beat complex ones- Why ML is not magic- How assumptions are tested- Model tracking conceptsTools Mentioned:- statsmodels- PyTorch- MLflow- Hugging Face7. Publishing Cadence- 1 issue per week or every 10 days- Rotate pillars8. What Is Out of Scope for Phase 1- Full website- Paid dashboards- Complex ML pipelines- Community features9. Success Metrics- Subscribers- Open rate- Replies- Tool requests10. Phase 1 Exit Criteria- 500+ engaged subscribers- Clear demand for tools- Strong performance in at least one pillarNorth Star Principle:Specific examples. Clear thinking. Transferable tools.
