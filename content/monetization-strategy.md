## Paid Tier Offerings

### Tier 1: "The Data Room" - $10/month
**For:** Casual fans who want more depth

- Full access to interactive dashboards (F1, Property)
- Weekly bonus analysis (the stuff that didn't make the main issue)
- Archive of all past analyses
- No ads (if you add sponsors later)

### Tier 2: "The Inner Circle" - $25/month
**For:** Serious enthusiasts and professionals

Everything in Tier 1, plus:
- Monthly deep-dive report (10+ pages)
- Access to raw data exports
- Templates (ROI calculators, analysis frameworks)
- Monthly Q&A / AMA session
- Private community (Discord/Slack)

### Tier 3: "The Formula" - $100/month (limited spots)
**For:** Power users, professionals, aspiring analysts

Everything in Tier 2, plus:
- 1-on-1 monthly call (15 min)

- Early access to new tools

## Launch Strategy

### Phase 1: Build Free Audience First (now â†’ 500 subs)
- Focus on free content quality
- Build trust and credibility
- Collect feedback on what people want
- Tease future paid features

### Phase 2: Soft Launch Paid Tier (500+ subs)
- Launch with founding member discount (50% off forever)
- Limited spots creates urgency
- Early supporters get lifetime perks
- Goal: 20-50 paid members

### Phase 3: Full Launch (1,000+ subs)
- Regular pricing
- Multiple tiers
- Referral program for paid members
- Goal: 5-10% conversion (50-100 paid members)

## Alternative Monetization (Not Subscriptions)

### Sponsorships
- Once you hit 1,000+ subs

- Only relevant brands (data tools, F1 merch, etc.)

- Mini-courses ($50-100)

### Consulting

## Key Insight

**Don't monetize too early.**

Focus on:
1. Building audience (500+ subscribers)
2. Proving value (high open rates, replies)
3. Understanding what people want (ask them!)
4. Creating assets worth paying for

Then monetize. Premature paywalls kill newsletters.
