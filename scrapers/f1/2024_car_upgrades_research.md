# F1 2024 Car Upgrades Research

## Overview
The 2024 F1 season was defined by dramatic car evolution, with McLaren going from midfield to constructors' champions through strategic upgrades.

---

## McLaren MCL38 - THE TRANSFORMATION STORY (Best for Animation)

McLaren's journey from backmarker to champion is the most visually dramatic story of 2024.

### Pre-Season (Bahrain - China): Rounds 1-5
- **Performance**: Average 19.2 points per race
- **Position**: 4th in constructors, ~0.5s behind Red Bull
- **Visual**: Original sidepods, standard floor design

### Miami GP Mega-Upgrade (Round 6) - MAY 5, 2024
**THE GAME CHANGER**
- **Components Changed**:
  - Brand new floor with redesigned leading edge
  - Hollowed-out lower front sidepods
  - New front and rear wings
  - New front suspension geometry
  - Redesigned engine cover
  - New sidepod inlets
  - Reshaped coke bottle section
  - Modified underfloor
- **Result**: Lando Norris's first F1 win
- **Performance Jump**: +0.5s per lap instantly
- **Visual Change**: Dramatic sidepod shape change - hollowed underneath

### Post-Miami (Rounds 6-24)
- **Performance**: Average 31.1 points per race (+62% improvement!)
- **Additional Upgrades**: Zandvoort (Round 15) refinements
- **Result**: CONSTRUCTORS' CHAMPIONS (first since 1998)

## Ferrari SF-24 - THE ROLLERCOASTER

### Launch Spec (Bahrain - Miami): Rounds 1-6
- Traditional sidepod design with lower lip inlet
- Competitive but inconsistent

### Imola Upgrade (Round 7) - MAY 19, 2024
- **Components Changed**:
  - New sidepod/floor combination
  - Red Bull-style upper lip radiator inlet (major visual change)
  - Greater undercut at front
  - More efficient coke bottle section
  - New diffuser
  - New front and rear wings
- **Visual Change**: Sidepod inlet shape completely different

### Barcelona Upgrade - BACKFIRED (Round 10) - JUNE 23, 2024
- **Components Changed**:
  - Evolved floor design
  - New diffuser
- **Problem**: Caused severe bouncing at high speed
- **Result**: Lost ground to McLaren, damaged title hopes

### Monza Recovery (Round 16) - SEPTEMBER 1, 2024
- **Components Changed**:
  - Completely revised floor
  - Fixed bouncing issues
- **Result**: Won 3 of final 9 races
- **Visual**: More refined sidepod/floor junction

### Singapore/Austin Front Wing (Rounds 18-19)
- New front wing design completed the recovery

---

## Red Bull RB20 - THE DECLINE

### Dominant Phase (Bahrain - Imola): Rounds 1-7
- **Performance**: Dominant, winning 5 of first 7 races
- **Visual**: Launch spec with signature cooling solution

### Struggle Phase (Miami - Hungary): Rounds 6-13
- Lost pace advantage as rivals caught up
- Hungary upgrade failed to deliver

### Recovery Attempts:

#### Baku (Round 17) - SEPTEMBER 15, 2024
- New underfloor and diffuser

#### Austin (Round 19) - OCTOBER 20, 2024
- New sidepods and engine cover

#### Las Vegas (Round 22) - NOVEMBER 23, 2024
- New venturi tunnel inlets
- Modified floor fences

### Special Note: Monza Low-Drag Package
- "Cooling cannons" on engine cover sides
- Unique low-downforce rear wing

---

## Mercedes W15 - THE REINVENTION

### Monaco Breakthrough (Round 8) - MAY 26, 2024
- **Abandoned** their unique front wing philosophy
- Adopted more conventional design
- **Result**: Won 3 of next 4 races

### Austin Recovery (Round 19)
- New sidepods blending multiple team philosophies
- Vertical openings and narrowed inlets

## RECOMMENDATION FOR ANIMATION

### Best Story: McLaren MCL38 Evolution
**Why**: Most dramatic visual transformation + best narrative (backmarker to champion)

**Three Key Stages to Animate**:
1. **Pre-Miami Spec** (Rounds 1-5): Original design
2. **Miami Mega-Upgrade** (Round 6): Hollowed sidepods, new floor
3. **Championship-Winning Spec** (Round 24): Refined final version

**Key Visual Differences**:
- Sidepod undercut (huge change)
- Floor edge design
- Engine cover shape
- Rear wing profile

### Alternative: Ferrari SF-24 Drama
**Three Stages**:
1. **Launch Spec**: Traditional sidepod inlet
2. **Imola Spec**: Red Bull-style upper lip inlet
3. **Monza Recovery Spec**: Refined floor/sidepod junction
